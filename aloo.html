Aloo Paratha Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 medium potatoes (boiled and mashed)
  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1-2 green chilies (finely chopped)
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (as needed)
  • Oil or ghee (for frying)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix the mashed potatoes with green chilies, cumin seeds, garam masala, and salt.
  2. In another bowl, knead the whole wheat flour with water to make a soft dough.
  3. Divide the dough and potato mixture into equal portions.
  4. Roll out one portion of dough into a circle, place a portion of the potato filling in the center, and seal the edges.
  5. Roll it out gently into a flat circle.
  6. Heat a tawa (griddle) and cook the paratha on both sides, applying oil or ghee until golden brown.
  7. Serve hot with yogurt or pickle.

Dietary Restrictions

This recipe is vegetarian and can be made vegan by using oil instead of ghee.
