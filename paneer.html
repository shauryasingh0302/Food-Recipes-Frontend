Paneer Butter Masala Recipe

Ingredients

  • 250g paneer (cubed)
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 large onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup cream
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Heat butter and oil in a pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter.
  2. Add chopped onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  3. Stir in ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute until fragrant.
  4. Add tomato puree and cook until the oil separates from the mixture.
  5. Mix in coriander powder, red chili powder, and salt. Cook for a few minutes.
  6. Add the paneer cubes and mix well, ensuring they're coated with the masala.
  7. Stir in cream and garam masala; cook for another 5 minutes.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice.

Dietary Restrictions

This recipe is vegetarian. For a vegan version, substitute paneer with tofu and use coconut cream instead of dairy cream.
